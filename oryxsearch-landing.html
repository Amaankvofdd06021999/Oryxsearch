How we work

A search process built on trust

We work as an extension of your team — learning your culture, your ambition, and the person who'll make the difference.

Step 01

Deep Discovery

We start by understanding your business, culture, and the real impact this role needs to make — not just a job description.

Step 02

Targeted Search

Leveraging a decade of relationships in deeptech and AI, we identify and approach the right candidates — not the obvious ones.

Step 03

Seamless Placement

We guide both sides through the process — from first conversation to signed offer — so the experience feels human at every step.
